What is the Standard Galactic Alphabet?

SGA is a fictional alphabet used in many classic video games, but it’s best known today from the enchantment table in Minecraft. While the text appears magical and unreadable, it follows a simple substitution cipher. Our tool reveals that mystery and lets you create your own!
